Twitter Social Mobility Index

A measure of social distancing derived from Twitter

Our Twitter derived measure of mobility in the United States -- a proxy for social mobility -- shows dramatic changes between before and after the start of COVID-19 social distancing measures. The above figure shows the mean variance in travel for Twitter users in the United States, as well as the states and cities with the highest/lowest reduction in mobility.

We derive this measure by monitoring public geolocated tweets to see how people are traveling. After the start of national social distancing policies (3/16/2020), people reduced movement by 55%. We can track these statistics nationally, by state and city. Check out our data and methods, and conduct your own analysis on factors that drive social distancing behaviors.
